REPO: tarot_card_aesthetic
PURPOSE: Tarot visual grammar — card format, symbolic vocabulary, from Rider-Waite through indie decks
STACK: Three.js + WebGL2 + GLSL + Vite

CARD FORMAT:
- Aspect ratio: 1:1.73 (portrait)
- Zones: outer_border / inner_margin / image_field (fills ~80%) / title_bar (bottom 10%)
- Number: top center, Roman numeral or Arabic
- Title: bottom, serif or ornate display font, centered

CORE MATH:
- Border pattern: SDF distance fields from card edges at multiple thresholds
- Gold shimmer: thin-film iridescence, hue_shift(dot(N,V)) * gold_intensity
- Star field: Poisson disk stars with 8-point cross flare
- Pip geometry: standard suit arrangements (1-10) packed into image field
- Symbol scatter: random placement with minimum separation constraint
- Sky gradient: vertical gradient in symbolic sky color

PALETTES:
- RIDER_WAITE: #FFF8E7, #FFD700, #8B0000, #191970, #228B22
- MARSEILLE_FLAT: #FF4500, #FFD700, #000080, #F5DEB3
- THOTH_JEWEL: #1C1C1C, #DAA520, #8B008B, #00008B
- DARK_ORACLE: #0D0D0D, #C0A870, #8B1C1C

REGIMES:
- RIDER_WAITE_REVIVAL: cream ground, heavy outline, gold accents, symbolic skies
- MARSEILLE_FLAT: geometric pips, flat color, decorative not illustrative
- THOTH_AETHYR: jewel colors on black, geometric/astrological abstraction
- INDIE_MINIMAL: white ground, hairline art, one accent color, plants+animals
- DARK_ORACLE: black ground, gold/aged-red marks, archaic urgency
